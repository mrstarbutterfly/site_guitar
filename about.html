Electro Guitars

Les Paul

Gibson Les Paul - the first electric guitar with a solid body from the company Gibson, one of the symbols of rock music and one of the most long-lived and popular models of musical instruments in the world. The model was developed in early 1950 by Ted McCarthy along with guitarist Les Paul.

Stratocaster

Fender Stratocaster (or Strat) is a model of electric guitar developed by George Fullerton, Leo Fender and Freddie Tavares in 1954 and produced up to the present. Stratocaster has been used by many guitarists, and therefore it can be heard on many historical records.
